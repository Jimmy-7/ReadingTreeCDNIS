THE FULL STORY


We are a GIN (Global Issues Network) club based in Hong Kong at the Canadian International School of Hong Kong. Each year our school travels to Bagan, Myanmar to visit and teach at the Basis Education Primary School.

The Reading Tree believes that education empowers individuals to lead positive social changes. Our vision is achieved through establishing a transferable English curriculum in order to support learning environments within the local and global community.

To achieve our vision, we created this website to facilitate easy access to our resources. We would like to invite other school clubs or organisations both locally based in Hong Kong and on an international platform to modify and use these resources for their use. We would only ask that you send us updates – pictures, stories, etc – of your organisation using our resources wherever you may need them!




OUR WORK


Since 2013, The Reading Tree has committed itself to helping poor communities in Myanmar with educating students on English, and raising fundings to help the people. Since then, we have expanded to include a Chiang Mai trip as well! Time away from these locations is used to plan lessons, curriculums, and activities for the children we have visited since.

Our sections Kids4Kids and Lilypad contribute to empowering the local community through education, helping local Hong Kong children and CDNIS younger students with English lessons respectively. Please see our other links for more information!
